    Throne of Shirvan

    A medieval Caspian economics game, c. 1100–1500

    You are the Shirvanshah, ruler of a Caspian-coast kingdom astride the Silk Road. For twenty-five years petitioners will reach your throne. Each will ask for something. Each decision will shift the kingdom. Keep five things in balance — your Treasury, your People, your Military, your Faith, your Naphtha — and your reign endures.

    • Treasury — your dinars and trade revenue.
    • People — welfare and loyalty.
    • Military — garrison and walls.
    • Faith — clerical favour and religious balance.
    • Naphtha — the Absheron oil reserves, your wildcard resource.
